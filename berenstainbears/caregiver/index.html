About the Berenstain Bears

Based on the best-selling book series, The Berenstain Bears is a charming animated comedy celebrating furry family values and good old-fashioned Bear Country living. Set in the rolling hills of Bear Country, the series stars a fun loving family of Bears whose love, loyalty and humor allow them to overcome any obstacle that finds its way down their sunny dirt road.

Even though life is pretty sweet for the honey loving Bear Family, Papa will often remark that "Sometimes there's trouble in Bear Country". Whether it's town politics, pollution, bullies, haunted lighthouses or just trying to fit in at school, the furry foursome are never far from adventure and intrigue.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who sings the theme song?
Lee Ann Womack performed the theme song.

Where can I buy videos and DVD's?
Berenstain Bears videos can be ordered from Sony Pictures Home Video.
They started release as of Jan 28th 2003.

Are there any live Berenstain Bears shows coming?
Nelvana does not have the rights for a live theatrical production. However, we are producing costumes and will have "meet and greet" events across Canada and the US for the Berenstain Bears at bookstores, malls and other similar venues. They will start in Spring 2003. For locations in the U.S. please contact you local PBS network for information.

How can I find out when the show airs?
Please contact your local PBS station in regards to their programming schedule. You can get a link to your local station through the PBS website. (http://pbskids.org) Go to "Find a PBS Station", click on the city closest to you. This will tell you what your local station is and will have a link to their website.

How can I get in touch with Jan and Stan Berenstain?
If you go to the Official Website for the book series at www.berenstainbears.com and click on "about the bears", scroll down to the bottom of the screen and you will see a place where you can email Jan and Stan Berenstain.

How old are Brother and Sister Bear?
Brother is 10 and Sister is 8.
