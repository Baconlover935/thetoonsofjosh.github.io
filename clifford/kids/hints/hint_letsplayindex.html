From this page, the child can get to any of Clifford's games and activities. In the "Help Clifford" area, the child can choose what Clifford needs to play at the beach, eat dinner, or engage in other activities. In "Clifford Kisses," matching colors earns characters a big, slurpy Clifford kiss. In "A Clifford Slide," matching colors and patterns earns characters a ride down Clifford's back. The mailbox icon links to the postcard page, where the child can e-mail a Clifford postcard to a friend. The crayon box icon links to Clifford coloring pages.
