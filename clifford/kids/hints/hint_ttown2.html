HINTS: Help the child look for clues in the pictures that lead to the correct answer. Which shop has bread in the window and other foods that are like bread? Which shop has eyeglasses in the window? Where else do you see eyeglasses at that shop? Which shop has pictures of dogs that have had a bath?

You may want to explain why incorrect choices are incorrect, based on the concepts of similarities and differences. Bread, rolls, cookies, and cake are alike because they are all foods, all made from flour, and all baked-but bread is very different from eyeglasses and dogs. All eyeglasses are alike in that they help people see better, even though they may be different sizes, colors, shapes, or strengths—but eyeglasses are very different from bread and dogs. Every dog is unique, but after a bath all dogs are alike in being clean-and dogs are very different from bread and eyeglasses.
