(Games marked + are more challenging.)

Ask the child what is happening in the picture. What would someone need to take a bath? What items would help Clifford?

Discuss why each of the other things would NOT help Clifford. (They would be useful in doing other things, but not in taking a bath. The wheelbarrow and rake would help Clifford work in the garden. The stepladder would help someone reach a high place-but Clifford wouldn't need it! The box of tools would help Clifford do many jobs around the house, but they wouldn't help him take a bath.)

Extend the activity offline by asking "What toys do you think Clifford likes to take into the bath with him?"
