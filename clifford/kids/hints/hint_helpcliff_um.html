HINTS: Ask the child what is happening in the picture. What would someone need when rain is falling? What item would help Clifford most?

You may want to explain that incorrect choices would not help Clifford in a rainstorm; they may be things that he would like to have, but they're not things that he would need.

Extend the activity offline by asking "How can you help me in the kitchen (or other area of the home)?" and "How can I help you?"
